About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8‑week live online program (starts 9 May 2025) focused on no‑code AI tools for automating documents, data analysis, and routine tasks.

Key Outcomes

  • Automate document, spreadsheet, and data workflows
  • Organize research knowledge and perform literature reviews with AI
  • Develop custom chatbots and AI‑powered dashboards
  • Create professional visuals and presentations using generative tools
  • Deliver a capstone AI solution
